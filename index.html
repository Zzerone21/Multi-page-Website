General Details

The country’s capital city is Seoul, South Korea. This is the largest city in the country and is officially named the Seoul Special City. Seoul is also the 5th largest metropolitan in the entire world. South Korea is located on the southern portion of the Korean peninsula, with the northern part belonging to North Korea. On the west side, the land meets the Yellow Sea and the Sea of Japan on the east. South Korea’s southern tip touches the East China Sea, mainly through the Korea Strait, connecting these three seas. The total area of South Korea is 100,210 square kilometers. It makes up about 45 percent of the peninsula’s land area. Filled with high mountain ranges, such as Seoraksan, and some narrow coastal plains. The Nakdong River, a famous place in South Korea, where you’ll find mountains and valleys in the southwestern region.
